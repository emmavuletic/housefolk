Terms & Conditions

Housefolk Terms of Service

Last updated: March 2026

1. Listing fees
Listing on Housefolk is currently free for landlords. Listings run for 7 days from their Thursday debut date.

2. Contacting landlords
Tenants create a free account to message landlords directly.

3. Thursday debut
All listings debut exclusively on Thursday when the newsletter sends to subscribers. Listings posted any day of the week will go live that Thursday.

4. Accuracy of listings
Landlords are responsible for the accuracy of their listings. Housefolk does not verify properties and accepts no liability for inaccurate information.

5. Service
Housefolk reserves the right to remove listings that violate these terms. Accounts may be suspended for misuse.

6. Prohibited content
Listings must be genuine UK properties. Fraudulent, misleading or illegal listings will be removed without refund.

To publish your listing, send the Thursday newsletter, and send enquiry notifications. We never sell your data.
